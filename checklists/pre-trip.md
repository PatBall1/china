# Pre-Trip Checklist

## Visa & entry
- [x] **Mainland China:** UK ordinary passport holders are **visa-free up to 30 days** (effective 17 Feb 2026, valid through 31 Dec 2026) for business/tourism/exchange/transit. This trip (~14 days) qualifies — **no visa needed**. *(Verify status close to departure as policy is a trial.)*
- [ ] **Passport validity:** at least **6 months** remaining beyond travel; blank pages available.
- [ ] **Hong Kong:** UK passport holders get visa-free entry (up to 180 days). Entering mainland from HK = a fresh visa-free entry — fine, well under 30 days.
- [ ] Carry evidence of purpose at the border: return/onward tickets, hotel bookings; HKU/Tsinghua invitation letters if available.
- [ ] Request HKU formal invitation letter (offered) if useful for reimbursement.

## Health & insurance
- [ ] Travel insurance covering China + medical.
- [ ] Check routine vaccinations; consult travel clinic for Yunnan (rural/tropical) if advised.
- [ ] Any prescriptions in original packaging + doctor's note.

## Money & connectivity
- [ ] Set up **Alipay** and/or **WeChat Pay** (essential in mainland China; link a card) before departure.
- [ ] Some CNY cash for arrival; conference hotel accepts Visa/Alipay/WeChat/cash.
- [ ] eSIM/roaming plan; consider a **VPN** installed *before* arrival for access to Google/work tools in mainland China.
- [ ] Download offline maps; note WeChat for local coordination.
