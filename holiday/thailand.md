# Holiday — Thailand (Bangkok + Gulf Islands)

Recommended ~6-night solo extension after Beijing, built for easy socialising and chilled beach fun. Default base: **Koh Samui (3 nights) + Koh Phangan (2 nights)**, with a Bangkok night on arrival. (Koh Tao excluded — been there.)

Provisional dates: depart Beijing **~8 Jul**, fly home **~14–15 Jul**. See [../flights.md](../flights.md) for the flight branch.

## Day by day (default)
- **Day 1 — Bangkok (1 night).** Land from Beijing (~6–7h). Stay Sukhumvit (Thonglor/Asok — rooftop bars, social) or Khao San area (backpacker, instant crowd). Ease in: street food + a rooftop or Khao San bar.
- **Day 2 — Bangkok → Koh Samui.** Quick flight (BKK→USM ~1h15, Bangkok Airways) or budget via Surat Thani + ferry. Afternoon on Chaweng/Chaweng Noi beach.
- **Days 2–4 — Koh Samui.** Beach by day, beach clubs + Chaweng nightlife by night. Easy to meet people; splash-friendly.
- **Day 5 — Samui → Koh Phangan.** Fast ferry (~30–45 min, Lomprayah/Seatran). Base at Haad Rin or chilled Srithanu/Thong Nai Pan.
- **Days 5–6 — Koh Phangan.** Chilled beaches/waterfalls by day; Half Moon / Jungle / Waterfall party by night if you fancy it (the big Full Moon is 29 Jul — outside this trip).
- **Day 7 — Home.** Fly Samui (USM) → London (via BKK/HKG/SIN), or ferry back to Surat Thani/BKK for a cheaper long-haul.

## Variants
- **Chill-leaning:** swap a Samui night for **Phangan's quiet north (Thong Nai Pan / Srithanu)** — yoga, wellness cafes, mellow beach bars; still social but laid-back.
- **Party-leaning:** more nights at **Chaweng (Samui)** + **Haad Rin (Phangan)**; time it for a Half Moon or Jungle Experience party night.
- **Shorter (4–5 nights):** skip Bangkok (fly Beijing→Samui via BKK same day) and split Samui + Phangan only.

## Where to meet people (low effort)
- **Social hostels/poshtels** with bars and events — book a few nights even if you splash on a villa later; the bar crawls and day-trips are where you meet people.
- **Beach clubs (Samui/Chaweng):** sunset sessions are easy mixers.
- **Group day-trips:** Angthong Marine Park boat tour, island bar crawls, snorkel/booze cruises — instant friend groups.
- **Phangan party nights:** Half Moon / Jungle Experience draw a sociable international crowd.

## Budget (per day, excl. flights)
- **Backpacker–mid:** £60–100 (hostel/3* + street food + a few drinks + ferries).
- **Comfortable:** £120–180 (nice hotel, beach clubs, taxis, day-trips).
- **Splash:** £200+ (beachfront villa/resort, speedboat transfers, bottle service).
- Rough 6-night on-ground total: **~£500 (mid) to ~£1,300 (splash)**.

## Logistics
- **Bangkok→Samui:** Bangkok Airways flies BKK→USM direct (~1h15, several daily). Budget option: AirAsia/Nok BKK→Surat Thani (~1h10) + bus/ferry combo (~£25, ~4–5h total).
- **Samui↔Phangan:** Lomprayah / Seatran high-speed ferries (~30–45 min, frequent).
- **Getting home:** Samui (USM) has 1-stop options to London (via BKK/SIN/HKG, ~16–18h). Cheaper to ferry back and fly from **Bangkok** long-haul.

## Safety / practical
- **Scooters:** rental is tempting but accidents + dodgy insurance are common — check your travel insurance covers it, wear a helmet, or use taxis/Grab.
- **Visa:** UK passports get visa-exempt entry to Thailand (long-standing). Confirm the current stay length on entry.
- **Money:** cash widely used on islands; ATMs charge ~฿220/withdrawal. Keep some baht for ferries/taxis.
- **Weather:** Gulf side is the dry side in July, but pack a light rain layer for brief showers.
- **Health:** the usual — stay hydrated, watch buckets on party nights, mind your drinks/belongings.
