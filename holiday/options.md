# Holiday Extension — Options

A post-Beijing solo beach trip (~8 Jul onward, 4 days to a week+), designed for an easy social scene (meet people without trying hard) and chilled beach fun, with a bit of cash to splash. Early–mid July weather and Beijing flight access factored in.

**Recommended: Thailand — Bangkok + Gulf islands (Koh Samui + Koh Phangan).** Detailed plan in [thailand.md](thailand.md).

Koh Tao and Bali are excluded from the top pick (been there) but Bali is kept below as a fallback.

| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |

⭐ = recommended.

## Why Thailand Gulf islands win for this brief
- **Lowest-effort socialising:** the island-hopping backpacker circuit means you're surrounded by other solo/young travellers; conversations happen by default. Big British presence.
- **Beach + fun balance:** chilled sand by day, lively bars/parties by night — your call each evening.
- **July timing:** the Gulf coast (Samui/Phangan) sits in a rain-shadow and is the driest part of Thailand this month, unlike the Andaman side (Phuket/Krabi) which is wet.
- **Cash to splash goes far:** upgrade to a beach-club villa, private speedboat transfer, or nice resort for a fraction of European prices.

## Notes
- Full Moon Party (Koh Phangan) next falls **29 Jul 2026** — outside this window — but Half Moon, Jungle Experience and Waterfall parties run through the month, so you still get the party option without it being the whole trip.
- Andaman-side Thailand (Phuket, Krabi, Phi Phi) is appealing but **wetter and rougher seas** in July — Gulf side is the smarter call this month.
- All of these assume you fly home from the holiday rather than Beijing on 7 Jul — see [../flights.md](../flights.md).
